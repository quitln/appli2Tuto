Danger of Cyclones

It is home to more than 11,000 plant and animal species, including more than a fifth of the fish tropical in the world and are the nesting sites of approximately 70% of the world's sea turtles. Cyclones are the most devastating natural phenomena: they affect the coastal zone ten times a year on average, destroy homes, crops and vegetation, contaminate fresh water supplies, and swollen rivers flood roads and areas housing.
