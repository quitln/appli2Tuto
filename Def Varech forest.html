A Varech forest

While there are large algae in all shapes and sizes, many look like to trees, with their long stems resembling flexible trunks called stipes. The kelp forest canopy is filled with flat leaf-like blades, with spikes that act like roots, anchoring algae to rocks. Some types of arctic algae can reach over ten meters and form large arches suspended in the sea. water column, with a shaded and protected understory. Like terrestrial forests, these marine forests provide habitat, breeding grounds and feeding grounds to many animals and fish, including cod, pollock, crab, lobster and sea urchin green.
