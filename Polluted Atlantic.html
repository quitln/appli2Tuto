Polluted Ocean

The Atlantic Ocean is the second largest body of water after the Pacific Ocean. Yet it is one of the most polluted oceans in the world. Indeed, it currently houses what we name the seventh continent which is a huge mass of garbage, mostly plastic,which floats on its surface. Ingestion of marine debris particularly affects sea turtles and seabirds, but it does not spare other marine mammals and fish. These generally swallow marine debris because they confuse them with prey. The seabed,especially near coastal regions, is also contaminated - mainly by plastic bags.
